The Big Drive
⚜ Lake Pontchartrain Causeway
The longest continuous bridge over water in the world — 24 miles across the lake. Halfway across you lose sight of land in every direction. It feels genuinely surreal. Pick up a rental car the morning of from a downtown location. Take I-10 west to Causeway Blvd in Metairie, then head north across to Mandeville. No stops or exits for the full 24 miles — fill up on gas and use the restroom before you get on.

Cash vs. Card
💳
Most restaurants take cards. Sit-down spots almost universally accept Visa, Mastercard, and Amex. Google Pay works at most modern terminals.
💵
Keep $60–100 cash on you at all times. Café Du Monde, Verti Marte, and many bars are cash only. Get cash at Rouses grocery store (Royal & St Peter) using debit card cashback — no ATM fee.
🍺
Bars are often cash only — especially the classic dives. Harry's Corner, Chart Room, and similar spots have ATMs on site. Have cash before walking into any unknown bar.
🥤
Open container laws are nonexistent in the Quarter. Take your drink outside in a plastic cup — no glass on the street. Most bars will swap your glass for a go-cup automatically.

Health & Comfort
💧
Hydrate constantly. Both on semaglutide which reduces thirst signals. In 90°F+ humidity you need more water than you think. Carry a bottle. Electrolyte packets worth packing.
🌡️
Heat strategy: Active sightseeing in the morning. Pool or indoors midday. Out again in the evening when it cools slightly. Late May averages 85–95°F with heavy humidity.
🌧️
Afternoon thunderstorms are nearly guaranteed in late May. They roll in fast and clear fast. Pack a compact umbrella or poncho.
🦟
Mosquitoes are real. Peak season in May. Bring bug spray. Any time near the river or in a courtyard at dusk, you'll feel them.
👟
Cobblestone is uneven. Comfortable shoes for both of you. For Toby's knee specifically — good supportive footwear is more important than style on the walking days.

Safety & Logistics
👜
Crossbody bag with a zipper. Crowded areas attract pickpockets. Keep phone and cards on your body, not in a tote or open bag.
🚕
Uber/Lyft surge hits hard on weekend nights. After 10pm expect to wait and pay more. Walk when you can — the Quarter is very walkable.
🏨
No room service at St. Marie. DoorDash and Uber Eats work great in the Quarter for late-night food. On-site restaurant and bar for everything else.
🌿
Powdered sugar warning. Beignets will cover you in white powder. Wear light colors for Café Du Monde or accept it as a New Orleans rite of passage.
🌉
Causeway tip: No stops or exits for 24 miles. Fill up on gas and use the restroom before you get on. $6 toll southbound only — cash or card.
